Mother Mary Teresa Bojaxhiu

The woman who devoted her life to caring for the sick and poor

Mother Teresa at a hospice for the destitute and dying in Kolkata (Calcutta), India, 1969

Here's a timeline of Mother Teresa

  • 1910 - Mother Teresa was born in Macedonia. She was named Agnes
  • 1928 - Agnes becomes a nun
  • 1931 - Sister Teresa becomes a teacher
  • 1937 - Sister Teresa is named Mother Teresa
  • 1957 - Mother Teresa opens an orphanage
  • 1969 - Mother Teresa establishes Missionaries of Charities
  • 1985 - The establishment of a center for people with AIDS.
  • 1997 - Mother Teresa dies
  • 2003 - Mother Teresa is named a saint
